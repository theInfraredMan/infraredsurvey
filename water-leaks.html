Coverage

Where We Look

From slabs to rooftops, we cover every surface where water hides. No guesswork — just thermal imaging that shows exactly where the problem is.

🏗️

Concrete Slabs

Slab leaks from plumbing lines create characteristic thermal hot or cool spots on the floor surface. We locate the pipe failure precisely — saving you from unnecessary concrete work.

🏠

Ceilings & Roof Penetrations

Water stains tell you where it drips — not where it enters. Thermal imaging traces the leak path back to the actual penetration point, even through multiple building layers.

🧱

Walls & Masonry

Moisture inside walls stays hidden until mold appears. We map wet insulation, saturated framing, and masonry infiltration before the damage becomes structural.

❄️

HVAC Condensate & Coils

Blocked condensate drains and leaking coil pans are a top source of hidden moisture. We identify HVAC-related water intrusion as part of any building survey.

🏢

Building Envelope

Window frames, curtain walls, and exterior joints let water in around the perimeter. Thermal imaging reveals infiltration pathways during and after rain events.

📑

Insurance Claims

Need documentation for a property damage claim? Our thermal report with detailed moisture mapping gives adjusters and attorneys exactly the objective evidence they need.
