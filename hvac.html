What We Inspect

Thermal Imaging
for Every System

Our certified thermographer captures what's invisible to the naked eye — identifying heat anomalies across all your critical equipment.

Data Center

CRAC Unit Inspection

Computer Room Air Conditioning units are mission-critical. Thermal scans reveal airflow imbalances, failing coils, and hotspots before they cause downtime.

HVAC

Rooftop Unit (RTU) Survey

Detect refrigerant leaks, compressor issues, and coil degradation in rooftop HVAC units — maximizing efficiency and extending equipment life.

Mechanical

Belt & Drive Inspection

Friction and misalignment generate heat long before a belt snaps. Thermal cameras catch wear patterns and tension issues during normal operation.

Boiler

Boiler Thermal Scan

Identify refractory deterioration, tube blockages, and insulation failures in boilers without taking them offline — keeping your facility running safely.

Boiler

Boiler Discharge Analysis

Thermal profiling of discharge lines pinpoints heat loss, valve leakage, and distribution inefficiencies — uncovering real energy savings.

HVAC

Coil Inspection

Fouled or damaged coils drive up energy costs and degrade air quality. Infrared imaging maps temperature distribution across the entire coil face.

Cleanroom

Laminar Flow Verification

Validate uniform airflow patterns in cleanrooms and critical environments. Thermal imaging reveals dead zones and turbulence that disrupt controlled conditions.
